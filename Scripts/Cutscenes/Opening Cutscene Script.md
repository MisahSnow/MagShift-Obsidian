THE DOMINION TRIALS Opening Cutscene Script Length: ~60 seconds

------------------------------------------------------------------------

0:00 – Establishing Shot

VISUAL A sprawling neon alien news studio floats in orbit. Massive
holographic screens surround the set showing:

-   Hovercars racing across alien landscapes
-   Exploding planets
-   Crowds cheering in enormous galactic arenas

A glowing logo rotates slowly:

THE DOMINION TRIALS

Energetic cyberpunk broadcast music plays. The camera pushes toward the
desk where two alien anchors sit.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

0:05 – Introduce Anchors

ANCHOR 1 – Male Alien (enthusiastic sports voice) “Good evening,
sentient viewers across the Dominion! I’m Krax Vell, and welcome back to
the most thrilling sporting event in the galaxy!”

ANCHOR 2 – Female Alien (cheerful but sharp) “And I’m Lyra‑9, coming to
you live from the Imperial Broadcast Nexus for another season of…”

The logo flashes dramatically.

ANCHORS (together) “THE DOMINION TRIALS!”

Crowd roar sound effect.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

0:12 – B‑Roll Racing Footage

VISUAL Hovercars scream through: - desert canyons - volcanic tracks -
toxic industrial cities

Closeups of drivers battling for position.

KRAX “For centuries, the Dominion has offered struggling civilizations a
generous opportunity!”

------------------------------------------------------------------------

0:18 – Dark Humor Moment

VISUAL A peaceful planet appears on screen. A red stamp flashes across
it:

ELIMINATED

Moments later a massive imperial fleet vaporizes it. The anchors
continue smiling.

LYRA‑9 “Compete in our high‑speed interplanetary racing championship…
and if your champion wins, your entire planet is spared!”

------------------------------------------------------------------------

0:27 – More B‑Roll

VISUAL Clips of past racers celebrating victory. Another screen shows
planets exploding.

KRAX “And if they lose…”

Beat.

KRAX “Well… statistically speaking, those civilizations won’t be
watching next season.”

Crowd laughter from the broadcast.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

0:35 – Introduce Current Season

VISUAL The camera pans across holographic displays showing competing
planets. Each icon flickers with stats and betting odds.

LYRA‑9 “This year’s Trials feature dozens of desperate worlds, billions
of viewers…”

KRAX “…and more corporate sponsors than ever before!”

Neon ads flash:

“EXTINCTION ENERGY DRINK” “BET YOUR PLANET TODAY”

------------------------------------------------------------------------

0:44 – Player Introduction

VISUAL One planet icon appears labeled:

UNKNOWN CIVILIZATION

A single racer silhouette appears.

LYRA‑9 “And representing a newly discovered world…”

The silhouette sharpens into the player’s hovercar.

LYRA‑9 “…their planet’s last hope.”

------------------------------------------------------------------------

0:50 – Stakes

VISUAL The player’s planet rotates slowly in space. Imperial warships
loom nearby.

KRAX “One racer.”

Cut to engines igniting.

KRAX “One machine.”

Cut to starting grid lights charging.

KRAX “One chance to avoid total planetary annihilation.”

------------------------------------------------------------------------

0:56 – Start of the Game

VISUAL The broadcast logo slams onto the screen:

THE DOMINION TRIALS

Crowds screaming.

LYRA‑9 “Engines are primed…”

KRAX “Betting is closed…”

Starting grid lights glow red.

ANCHORS (together) “Let the Trials begin!”

------------------------------------------------------------------------

1:00 – Gameplay Transition

Lights turn GREEN. Hovercars launch forward. Camera snaps into the
player’s perspective.

GAMEPLAY STARTS
